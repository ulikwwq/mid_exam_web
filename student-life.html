Tech Club

The Tech Club focuses on coding challenges, robotics projects, hackathons, and collaborative tech activities. Students gain hands-on experience in software and hardware projects.

Innovation Club

The Innovation Club encourages students to explore STEM concepts, create innovative projects, and participate in maker activities and competitions.

Annual Hackathon

The Annual Hackathon brings students together to solve real-world challenges within 48 hours. Teams collaborate and innovate under time constraints.

Cultural Festival

The Cultural Festival celebrates diversity on campus through music, art, performances, and interactive activities organized by students.

Cybersecurity Program

The Cybersecurity program trains students to secure networks, prevent cyber attacks, and implement advanced protection strategies. Students will work on real-world projects, penetration testing, and threat analysis simulations.

  • Network Security
  • Ethical Hacking & Pen Testing
  • Cryptography & Data Protection
  • Incident Response & Risk Management
STEM Pedagogy Program

The STEM Pedagogy program focuses on effective teaching strategies for STEM subjects, promoting hands-on learning and critical thinking among students.

IT & Business Program

The IT & Business program equips students with combined technical and business skills, preparing them to solve real-world business challenges using technology.

Software Engineering Program

The Software Engineering program teaches modern software development practices, design patterns, collaborative project management, and real-world application deployment.
